CLASS:
chair

ATTRIBUTES:
color (string)           // color of the chair
height (float)           // height of the chair
fold (boolean)           // is the chair folded?
material (string)        // material chair is made out of

METHODS:
change_color (changes the chair's color)
raise_height (raises the chair's height)
lower_height (lowers the chair's height)
verify_material (returns the type of material the chair is made out of)
re_fold (folds chair into storage state)
un_fold (folds chair into a sitting state)
