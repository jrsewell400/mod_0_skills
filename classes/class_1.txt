CLASS:
table

ATTRIBUTES:
color (string)           // color of the table
height (float)           // height of the table
seatCapacity (integer)   // how many people can be seated at the table
material (string)        // material table is made out of

METHODS:
change_color (changes the table's color)
raise_height (raises the table's height)
lower_height (lowers the table's height)
verify_material (returns the type of material the table is made out of)
reduce_seatCapacity (reduces the amount of people able to be seated at the table)
increase_seatCapacity (increases the amount of people able to be seated at the table)
